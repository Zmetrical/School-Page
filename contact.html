Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common inquiries we receive. Can't find what you need? Send us a message above.

When is the enrollment period for the next school year?
Enrollment schedules are announced through our official Facebook page and via announcements posted on campus. You may also call us at (+63) 919-893-4789 to confirm the latest enrollment dates.
What documents do I need to enroll?
Generally, incoming Grade 11 students are required to submit a PSA Birth Certificate, Form 138 (Report Card) from their previous school, Certificate of Good Moral Character, and 2×2 ID photos. Requirements may vary — please contact the school or visit the Admissions page for the full list.
Are there available scholarships or financial assistance programs?
Yes! TPC participates in various government scholarship programs including the Senior High School Voucher Program. We also have institutional freebies and other assistance options. Visit our Scholarships & Freebies page or call us for more details.
How do I request school documents (Form 137, Diploma, etc.)?
You may visit the school registrar's office in person, or submit a request through our Document Request page. Processing time varies depending on the type of document. Please allow at least 3–5 school days for standard requests.
What strands / tracks does TPC Marilao offer?
TPC Marilao offers both Academic and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) tracks. Visit our Programs page for the full list of available strands and course descriptions.
What are the school's office hours?
The school office is open Monday to Friday, 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays. For urgent concerns outside office hours, you may message us on Facebook.

Overview

Trinity Polytechnic College — Marilao

Trinity Polytechnic College (TPC) is a DepEd-recognized private educational institution located in Ibayo, Marilao, Bulacan. Founded in 1989, TPC has grown into a trusted center of quality and affordable Senior High School education, offering both academic and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) tracks designed to prepare students for higher education, employment, and entrepreneurship.

Rooted in the belief that every student deserves access to excellent education regardless of financial standing, TPC has remained steadfast in its mission to nurture the mind, body, and spirit of every learner. The college is guided by its core motto — Unitas, Excellentia, Caritas — which embodies the values of unity, excellence, and charity that define the TPC community.

Through rigorous academic programs, values formation, and active community engagement, TPC continues to produce competent, God-centered, and socially responsible graduates who make a positive difference in their families, communities, and the broader society.

What We Stand For

Core Values

At the heart of Trinity Polytechnic College are three guiding principles that shape our culture, our decisions, and the character of every graduate we produce.

Unitas

Unity

We foster a sense of family and belonging, celebrating diversity while building a strong, unified community of learners, educators, and stakeholders.

Excellentia

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in all academic and personal endeavors, encouraging every member of the TPC community to strive for their best.

Caritas

Charity

We cultivate a spirit of genuine service and compassion toward God, our community, and our country — extending care beyond the classroom.

Direction

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Trinity Polytechnic College envisions itself to be a leading institution that fosters academic excellence, moral formation, and holistic development — committed to producing competent, God-centered, and socially responsible graduates prepared for the challenges of a global society.

Our Mission

TPC is committed to providing quality and affordable education through rigorous academic programs, values formation, and community engagement — nurturing the mind, body, and spirit of every student to become productive members of society.

Our Commitment

Institutional Quality Policy

Trinity Polytechnic College is committed to delivering meaningful basic education that emphasizes academic excellence, holistic character development, and spiritual growth.

We pledge to continuously improve our processes, programs, and outcomes to consistently meet and exceed the expectations of our students, parents, and the broader community we serve.

Guided by our core values of Unitas, Excellentia, and Caritas, every member of the TPC community — administration, faculty, and staff — actively participates in upholding this quality commitment in all aspects of institutional life.
